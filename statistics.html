What's happening?

People continue to leave Venezuela to flee violence, insecurity and threats, as well as the lack of food, medicines and essential services. With more than 4 million Venezuelans and Venezuelans living abroad, the vast majority in South American countries, this is the largest exodus in the region's recent history.
